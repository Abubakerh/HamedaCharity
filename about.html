Aidan Al Mutassit is a charity that is dedicated to helping people in need.

We are committed to providing food, shelter, and education to those who are less fortunate. We also work to promote peace and understanding between different cultures.

Our charity was founded in 2023 by Aidan Al Mutassit, a young entrepreneur who is passionate about helping others. Aidan has seen the firsthand effects of poverty and inequality, and he is determined to make a difference in the world.

At Aidan Al Mutassit, we believe that everyone deserves a chance to succeed. We are committed to helping people reach their full potential. We provide a variety of programs and services that help people to improve their lives.

Our programs and services include:

  • Food assistance
  • Shelter assistance
  • Educational assistance
  • Vocational training
  • Job placement assistance
  • Medical assistance
  • Mental health assistance
  • Disaster relief
  • Peacebuilding and conflict resolution programs
